About Karupatti Shop

Our Story

Karupatti Shop is a multi-vendor e-commerce platform dedicated to bringing you authentic, high-quality products from trusted sellers. We specialize in traditional products like Karupatti (palm jaggery) and other natural, minimally processed goods that celebrate heritage and wellness.

What is Karupatti?

Karupatti, also known as palm jaggery, is a traditional natural sweetener made from the sap of palm trees. Unlike refined sugar, it retains essential minerals and nutrients, making it a healthier alternative. Our platform connects you with authentic sellers who source and produce genuine Karupatti products.
